Privacy Policy for Vlocity CPQ Random Data Generator Chrome Extension

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Vlocity CPQ Random Data Generator Chrome Extension (the "Extension").

Information We Collect

The Vlocity CPQ Random Data Generator Chrome Extension does not collect any personally identifiable information from users. However, it may collect and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Salesforce SID (Session ID) for calling OOTB Salesforce and Vlocity APIs, it is stored in Chrome Local Storage.
  • Settings and preferences chosen by the user within the extension.

Data Encryption

The Vlocity CPQ Random Data Generator Chrome Extension utilizes encryption to secure the storage of usernames and passwords provided by users. This encryption ensures that sensitive information is protected from unauthorized access or disclosure. The encrypted data is stored locally in the Chrome storage and is not accessible to anyone other than the user who provided it.

How We Use Information

The information collected and stored by the Vlocity CPQ Random Data Generator Chrome Extension is solely used for the purpose of facilitating the login process to Salesforce accounts. This information is stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted or shared with any third parties.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of the information collected and stored by the Vlocity CPQ Random Data Generator Chrome Extension. This includes implementing encryption and other security protocols to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

No Data is stored on cloud, everything is on your Chrome Local Storage.
